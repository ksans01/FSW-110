About Us

All of our Little Dinosaurs are pasture raised on over 10 acres of land!

We allow our Little Dinosaurs to breed on their own and raise their young. This gives us the most natural, happy Dinos possible!

Because of this we have an abundance of Roosters. Get one today to protect your own flock, or to put on your dinner table!

Beautifully assorted egg colors! You'll ALMOST not want to crack them open!
